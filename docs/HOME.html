A small (and growing) library of open-source scripts, assets and resources for Roblox Developers

A GUIDE TO WHAT WE DO

We offer high quality scripts and assets for all forms of developers, you do not need to credit us, unless otherwise stated in the script. Most of the scripts that we offer are rather basic, but over time, we will begin to offer more complex and high quality assets for more experienced developers!

Some of our assets are based around roleplay-type games but most of our assets can be used on any type of game!

WE ARE A NON-PROFIT SERVICE

We are entirely a non-profit development service, this is to help new and experienced developers access the resouces that they need to make a sucessful game without having to spend hours learning how to make it or having to sort through all of the virus-ridden models in the roblox library.

ALL OF OUR ASSETS ARE OPEN SOURCE

All of the resources that we offer are open source and none of our scripts are obfuscated. We do this so that you both know what is in the scripts and you can also learn from it and understand how it works so you can
